About Me

Hi! I'm Ellie. I'm a student at the University of Michigan minoring in User Experience and Design. I'm currently a junior and I'm planning to graduate in 2025. I'm super passionate and excited about web design and accessibility. This project is for my SI339 Intro to Web Design class. Like I mentioned before, I was born and raised in Grand Rapids, MI. I love this little city, and I made this site to share all my favorite spots with you. I also want to use it as a portfolio to keep track of my favorite spots, so I can look back any time. I'm a cheerleader at the Univeristy of Michigan. I enjoy supporting Michigan Athletics and bringing spirit to Michigan events. Other than school, I like jigsaw puzzles, running, hanging out with my dogs, going out to eat, and traveling. I'm studying abroad in Europe next semester, so you might catch a travel blog if you stick around.
